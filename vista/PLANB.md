## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| College says no to pilot | Blocks Phase 2 | Have backup college. Pitch benefits: free system, no cost to them. |
| Students refuse consent | Can't enroll faces | Explain data stays on-premise, not cloud. Get HOD endorsement. |
| Paper rejected | No publication credit | Submit to 2-3 conferences simultaneously (different papers). |
| Accuracy drops on real 30 students | Can't claim results | Threshold tuning + enrollment quality control |
| Team members leave after graduation | Loses manpower | Document everything. System is self-contained. |
| Funding application rejected | No money | Apply to 3-5 programs, not just one. |
