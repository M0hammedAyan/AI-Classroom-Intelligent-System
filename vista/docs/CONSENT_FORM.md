### Purpose of Study

We are conducting a pilot study to test an AI-based attendance system that uses face
recognition technology to mark student attendance automatically from classroom photographs.

### What We Collect

- **3-5 facial photographs** of you (used to create a mathematical face embedding)
- **Attendance records** (which days you were present/absent)
- **No other biometric data** is collected

### How Your Data is Used

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Face photos | Create face embedding only — photos deleted after processing | Local server |
| Face embedding | Match against classroom photo for attendance | Encrypted database |
| Attendance | Track presence for pilot validation | Local database |

### Key Points

1. **Local only** — Your data NEVER leaves the college server. No cloud storage.
2. **Not shared** — Your data is not shared with any third party.
3. **Deletable** — You can request deletion of your data at any time.
4. **Voluntary** — Participation is completely voluntary. Non-participation has NO impact on your grades or standing.
5. **Withdrawal** — You may withdraw from the study at any time without explanation.
6. **Duration** — Data will be retained for the duration of the pilot (8 weeks) + analysis period (4 weeks). After that, all data is permanently deleted unless you consent to continued use.

### Risks

- Minimal risk. Face recognition technology may occasionally misidentify (false match). All automated attendance is verified by the teacher.
- No risk to academic standing regardless of participation.
