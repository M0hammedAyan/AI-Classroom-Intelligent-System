### What We're Requesting

Permission to deploy VISTA — our AI-powered attendance and student risk prediction system

### What It Does

- Marks attendance automatically from a single classroom photograph (face recognition)
- Predicts which students are at risk of failing using attendance + score patterns
- Alerts mentors before problems become irreversible
- Provides dashboards for teacher, mentor, HOP, and students
